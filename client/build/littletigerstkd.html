Little Tigers Have Fun In A Safe Environment

We understand the unique challenges preschool and kindergarten age children present to both parents and teachers. Our Little Tigers martial arts classes are specially designed for young children to keep them excited to come to class every time. Plus, your child will gain greater focus, which will carry over to home and school as well.

Little Tigers Learn and Grow

  • Kids are given a Belt and Uniform of their own
  • Kids are taught to bow and greet instructors
  • Kids are taught to answer with "Yes, sir" and "Yes, ma'am"
  • Kids are taught to sit quietly on the mat before class begins
  • Kids are taught to keep their partners safe
  • Kids are taught not to goof off in locker rooms or during class
  • Kids are taught to look and listen to instructors
  • Kids are taught to always bow before stepping on and off the mat
